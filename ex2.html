Gianni Versace usually referred to as Versace is an Italian luxury elite fashion company founded by Gianni Versace in 1978. The company produces Italian-made ready-to-wear and accessories, as well as haute couture under its Atelier Versace brand and licenses its name and branding to Luxottica for eyewear. Gianni Versace being a native of Calabria, in the ancient Magna Graecia (Greater Greece), the company logo is inspired by Medusa, a figure from Greek mythology.

Giorgio Armani is an Italian luxury fashion house founded by Giorgio Armani in 1975. Known for its clean, tailored lines and neutral color palette, the company designs and manufactures haute couture, ready-to-wear, leather goods, shoes, watches, jewelry, accessories, eyewear, cosmetics, and home interiors. The brand is recognized worldwide for its understated elegance and timeless style.
